Novel

information

Yassin began writing the novel on June 8, 2022, and it is still continuing to this day. It now contains 4 chapters. The author released a preliminary copy of the book and released the title a few days ago, which is “Is it really my fault?”

summary

"Jhone is a murderer in his final moments as he heads towards the execution platform where his head will be severed in front of the entire world, marking him as the most dangerous criminal in America's history. The novel takes us inside Jhone's mind, revealing the reasons behind his transformation into a ruthless killer and the psychological changes that turned his heart black

is it really my fault?”

He lived a life of solitude and disappointment without a father, left by his father with his addicted mother at a young age. Several experiences thereafter convinced him that there is no way to eliminate evil except through evil itself. He believed that there is no way for human nature to remain pure without punishment. The story explores how one's true nature is revealed when faced with death. Jhone decides to purify the world by eliminating those who immerse themselves in it, engaging in an intellectual war against misguided concepts and laws that lack a basis of truth. All of this and more can be found in the novel
